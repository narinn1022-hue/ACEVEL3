Singapore

Highlight

Singapore is a prosperous island city-state in Southeast Asia, known for its strategic location, bustling port, and multicultural society.

HSBC Rain Vortex

The world's tallest indoor waterfall surrounded by a multi-level forest.

Butterfly Garden

A lush garden with more than 40 species of butterflies in Terminal 3.

Changi Experience Studio

Exhibition on airport history through fun games and simulations.

Philippines

Highlight

The Philippines is an archipelagic country comprised of over 7,000 islands with a diverse culture shaped by indigenous and Spanish influences.

Rizal Park

A 58-hectare park dedicated to José Rizal, the national hero of Philippines.

Fort Santiago

A historic defensive fortress within the walled city of Intramuros.

SM Mall of Asia

One of the largest shopping malls in the world, featuring an IMAX theater.

Thailand

Highlight

Thailand is the only Southeast Asian nation never colonized, famous for its vibrant culture, food, and stunning landscapes.

The Erawan Museum

Famous for its giant three-headed bronze elephant statue atop the building.

The Ancient City

World's largest open-air museum with replicas of Thai historical buildings.

Lat Krabang Park

An ideal city park for walking and jogging near Suvarnabhumi Airport.
